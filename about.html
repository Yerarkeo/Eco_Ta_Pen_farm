Welcome to Ta Pen Eco Farm

At Ta Pen Eco Farm, crops are grown with natural compost and organic pest control, while chickens roam freely. Trees are also planted to protect the soil and environment.

Mission

Ta Pen Eco Farm's mission is to promote public health by growing safe, chemical-free fruits and vegetables, while protecting the environment through eco-friendly practices such as compost use, solar-powered irrigation, natural pest control, and biodiversity preservation. The farm also seeks to support the local community, encourage sustainable living, and develop future opportunities for education and eco-tourism.

Values

Ta Pen Eco Farm values health, sustainability, nature care, and community support. The farm is committed to providing safe, chemical-free food, using natural methods to protect soil and water, and relying on solar energy. It seeks to live in harmony with biodiversity by planting trees and preserving the environment, while also supporting local farmers and inspiring the community toward eco-friendly living.
