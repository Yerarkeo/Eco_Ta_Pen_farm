Welcome to Ta Pen Eco Farm

Gauvas harvest

Pick guavas when they turn light green to yellow and give off a sweet aroma.

Guavas Care

Water guava trees deeply once a week and prune to keep good shape.

Plant Banana

Water regularly and keep soil moist to help banana plants grow healthy.

Banana Harvest

Harvest bananas when the fruits are plump, slightly rounded, and the edges lose their sharp angles.

Plant Papaya

Water papaya regularly and add compost for healthy growth.

Papaya Harvest

Harvest papayas when the skin turns yellow-orange and feels slightly soft.

Oranges Take Care

Keep lemon trees well-drained, fertilized, and in sunny spots.

Havest Oranges

Gather lemons when they are fully yellow and easy to twist off the branch.

Flower Care

Water flowers regularly, provide sunlight, and remove dead blooms.

Havest Flower

Pick flowers when petals are fully open but not wilted.

Care Chickens

Provide clean water, nutritious feed, and a safe, clean coop for chickens.

Sell Chickens

Collect eggs daily when they are fully formed; for meat, harvest when chickens reach desired size.

Ta Pen Eco Farm values health, sustainability, nature, and community. It grows safe food, protects the environment, and supports local farmers.

Ta Pen Eco Farm (2020) grows chemical-free crops and raises free-range chickens, selling products locally.
