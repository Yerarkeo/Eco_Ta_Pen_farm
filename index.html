Welcome to Ta Pen Eco Farm

Your destination for organic farming and eco-friendly practices.

Eco Ta Pen Farm is dedicated to sustainable agriculture and providing fresh, organic produce to our community. We believe in eco-friendly farming practices that nurture the environment.

Ta Pen Eco Farm grows guava as one of its main crops, using only natural compost and eco-friendly methods. The guava is fresh, chemical-free, and rich in vitamin C and fiber, making it healthy for consumers. By producing guava this way, the farm promotes good health and protects the environment.

Our Activities
